What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a tool used to estimate a person's body fat based on their weight and height. Introduced by Adolphe Quetelet in the 19th century, BMI helps categorize individuals into various weight categories such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obese.
